## Executive Summary

Analysis of the top 5 baby sleep consultants reveals a market dominated by:
- **Fear-based messaging** (attachment damage, long-term harm)
- **Quick-fix solutions** vs. sustainable approaches
- **Science-based authority positioning**
- **Exhausted parent pain points** ("tired AF", sleep-deprived)
- **Gentle vs. traditional sleep training** debate

**HLS COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE:** "Parental Wellbeing First" positioning shifts the narrative from child-only focus and parental guilt to parent empowerment, self-care as foundation, and responsive (not rigid) approaches.
