## How It Works

### The 5-Email Flow

1. **Email 1 (Immediate)**: Welcome + Jade's brand story
   - Goal: Build connection and trust
   - Copy: Personal, warm, establishes values

2. **Email 2 (Day 2)**: Free value (sleep guide sample)
   - Goal: Deliver immediate value, prove expertise
   - Copy: Gives away something useful, positions for next email

3. **Email 3 (Day 5)**: Problem/Solution positioning
   - Goal: Identify pain, position guides as answer
   - Copy: "Here's what I see most... that's why I built guides"

4. **Email 4 (Day 8)**: Social proof + soft pitch
   - Goal: Remove doubt with testimonials, introduce offer
   - Copy: Real customer quotes, pricing, no pressure

5. **Email 5 (Day 12)**: Objection handling + final CTA
   - Goal: Answer last questions, drive conversion
   - Copy: FAQ format, money-back guarantee, clear CTA

**Why this timing?**
- Not too fast (respects inbox)
- Not too slow (maintains momentum)
- Adjustable based on your preference
