### What Makes HLS Emails DIFFERENT

**1. Parental Wellbeing is PRIMARY (Not Secondary)**

**Competitor Approach:**  
- Focus on baby sleep outcomes
- Parent wellbeing mentioned as side benefit

**HLS Approach:**

- "Your sleep matters as much as baby's"

- Permission to prioritize rest without guilt

**Email Example:**

**Why This Matters:**  
UNCLAIMED GAP. No competitor centers parental wellbeing this strongly. This resonates with exhausted parents who feel guilty for needing rest.

---

**2. Vulnerability BEFORE Advice (Founder Authenticity)**

**Competitor Approach:**  
- Expert-to-parent dynamic
- Minimal personal story
- Professional credibility first

**HLS Approach:**  
- Jade shares her struggles FIRST (4-month regression broke her, partner fights, feeling like a failure)

**Competitor Approach:**

**Why This Matters:**

**Competitor Approach:**  
- Barely mentioned
- Assumed both parents are aligned
- Focus on baby/parent dyad only

**HLS Approach:**  
- Full section in guides dedicated to partner alignment
- Email specifically about getting on the same page
- Acknowledges partner conflict as MAJOR barrier to sleep success
- Conversation frameworks, fair distribution of night duties
- "The method doesn't matter if you're not on the same page"

**Why This Matters:**  
UNCLAIMED GAP. This is a massive pain point that NO competitor addresses deeply. Parents dealing with partner misalignment will convert at high rates because no one else is solving this.

**5. Communication, Not Crying (Responsive Language)**

**Competitor Approach:**  
- "Sleep training" language (fixing a problem)

**HLS Approach:**
